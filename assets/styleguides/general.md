# General Conductor Styleguide

## Intent

- Prefer explicit, reviewable workflow artifacts over hidden assumptions.
- Keep specs outcome-oriented and plans implementation-oriented.
- Update the smallest set of Conductor files needed to keep the track coherent.

## Writing Rules

- Write in concise Markdown.
- Prefer stable headings over ad hoc prose.
- Record real decisions and real remaining gaps; do not leave placeholder claims after work starts.
